Hello class! This is a working demo of an automatically scrolling div. Notice how many scripts are linked in the head! This is a classic example of frankensteining together your desired functionality out of many open source libraries. In this, it is a library called jQuery Serial Scroll written by Ariel Flesler (who I believe has contributed extensively to the jquery project). For class, try to add your own content to the scrolling section without breaking it! If that works, try copying it, to have two on the page, or try re-styling the text. I'm excited to see what you all make!
